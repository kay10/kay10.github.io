Co-Founder & CTO, Crosscope

Biography

Ketan Bacchuwar is Co-Founder & CTO of Crosscope. With 9 years of experience, his strengths shine when bridging the valley-of-death, by translating state-of-the-art artificially intelligent image processing algorithms to practical and usable solutions for industrial challenges. Ketan is excited about bridging health disparities with engineering strategies using new technologies.
